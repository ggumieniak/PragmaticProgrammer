#  Tortoise
---
1. User give the simple instructions as:
    * P 2 - get second pen 
    * D - draw
    * W 2 - west 2 cm 
    * N 1 - 
    * E 2
    * S 1
    * U
    
## Dictionary
* CM - Means the length of one character
* P - Pen
* D - Draw
* W - West
* N - North
* E - East
* S - South
* U - Pick up a pen
